Welcome to the Elmo Fan Site!

1972:
Elmo was first created in 1972 and appeared on the show Sesame Street as a baby monster.

1984:
Elmo's character was brought to life one day in 1984 by a talented puppeteer known as Kevin Clash. From then on Kevin would continue to perform as Elmo until 2012.

1996:
The "Tickle Me Elmo" toy was released in 1996 and was the most popular Elmo toy. It was released during the holiday season.

1998:
It was at this time that Elmo recieved his own segment on Sesame Street called 'Elmo's World.' It is a 20 minute segment.

2012:
Kevin Clash resigned from the Sesame Workshop and would no longer go on to perform as Elmo. From 2012 to present Ryan Dillon has taken over as Elmo's puppeteer.
